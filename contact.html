Report Bugs & Suggest Features

Found a bug or have an idea for a new feature? We want to hear about it!

When reporting bugs, please include:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What actually happened
  • Your browser and operating system
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Steps to reproduce the issue

For feature suggestions:

  • Describe the feature and why it would be useful
  • Explain how it would improve your experience
  • Share any examples from other platforms if relevant

Copyright & DMCA Notices

If you believe content on Dirty4 infringes your copyright:

  1. Contact the original source (Danbooru, Safebooru, etc.) to request removal
  2. Email us at legal@dirty4.com to exclude the content from our platform
  3. Include the specific URL and proof of ownership

We take copyright seriously and will respond to valid DMCA notices within 2-5 business days.
