Getting Started

What is Dirty4?
Dirty4 is a modern, mobile-optimized anime art gallery and archival platform. We aggregate artwork from public sources like Danbooru and Safebooru, providing a beautiful, fast interface for browsing and discovering digital art.
Do I need to create an account?
No! Dirty4 works without requiring an account. All your preferences, starred items, and search history are stored locally in your browser. This means better privacy and faster performance.
How do I start browsing?
Simply go to the Gallery App and start exploring! You can:
  • Browse the main feed for popular artwork
  • Use the search bar to find specific content
  • Click on tags to discover related artwork
  • Star items to save them to your favorites
Is Dirty4 free to use?
Yes! Dirty4 is completely free. We display ads through Google AdSense to support development and hosting costs, but all features are available to everyone at no charge.

Search & Tags

How do I search for artwork?
Use the search bar at the top of the gallery. You can search by:
  • Tags: Enter keywords like "anime girl" or "cyberpunk"
  • Multiple tags: Combine tags with spaces: anime landscape sunset
  • Specific characters: Search character names directly
Pro Tip: The search has autocomplete! Start typing and suggestions will appear to help you find the right tags.
What are tags?
Tags are keywords that describe artwork. They can include:
  • Characters: Character names and franchises
  • Styles: Anime, manga, realistic, chibi, etc.
  • Elements: Objects, settings, actions in the image
  • Artists: Creator names
  • Meta tags: Quality, resolution, aspect ratio
Learn more in our Guide to Understanding Art Tags.
How do advanced search operators work?
Use these operators for precise searches:
  • tag1 tag2 - Images with BOTH tags (AND)
  • ~tag1 ~tag2 - Images with EITHER tag (OR)
  • -tag - Exclude images with this tag (NOT)
  • tag* - Wildcard matching (finds tag, tags, tagging, etc.)
Example: anime girl -blonde finds anime girls without blonde hair.

Features

How do I save favorite artwork?
Click the star icon on any image to add it to your favorites. Starred items are saved locally in your browser and accessible through the "Starred" tab. Your favorites persist across sessions until you clear your browser data.
Can I download images?
Yes! Click on an image to view it full-size, then use the download button. Images are sourced from public repositories like Danbooru and Safebooru. Please respect copyright and use downloaded images responsibly.
Copyright Notice: Downloaded images are for personal use only unless you have permission from the artist. Do not redistribute or use commercially without proper licensing.
What's the difference between sources?
Dirty4 aggregates from multiple sources:
  • Danbooru: Large, comprehensive imageboard with detailed tagging
  • Safebooru: Family-friendly version with filtered content
  • Other galleries: Additional public art repositories
You can filter by source in the gallery settings.

Mobile Usage

Is Dirty4 mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! Dirty4 is designed with mobile-first principles. Our glassmorphism UI is optimized for one-handed browsing with intuitive gestures and thumb-friendly navigation.
Can I install Dirty4 as an app?
Yes! Dirty4 is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it to your home screen:
  • iOS: Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen
  • Android: Chrome → Menu → Install App
  • Desktop: Look for the install button in your browser's address bar
Installed apps work offline and provide a native app-like experience!
What mobile gestures are supported?
  • Swipe left/right: Navigate between images
  • Tap: View full-size image
  • Long press: Quick actions menu
  • Pinch to zoom: Zoom in on details
  • Pull to refresh: Reload current view

Troubleshooting

Images aren't loading. What should I do?
Try these solutions:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Disable browser extensions that might block images
  • Try a different browser or device
If problems persist, the source server (Danbooru/Safebooru) might be temporarily down.
Search isn't working properly
  • Check spelling - tags must match exactly
  • Use autocomplete suggestions
  • Try broader search terms
  • Remove special characters
  • Clear your browser cache
I lost my starred items!
Starred items are stored in your browser's local storage. You may have lost them if you:
  • Cleared your browser data/cookies
  • Used incognito/private mode
  • Switched browsers or devices
  • Used a browser cleaner tool
Prevention Tip: Regularly export your starred items (feature coming soon) or take screenshots of important artwork.
The site is slow or laggy
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Disable heavy browser extensions
  • Update your browser to the latest version
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
  • Check if your device has sufficient RAM available
  • Reduce image quality in settings (if available)

Privacy & Security

What data does Dirty4 collect?
We collect minimal data:
  • Local Storage: Preferences, starred items, search history (stored on YOUR device)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the platform
  • Cookies: For functionality and advertising (via Google AdSense)
We do NOT collect or store personal information. Read our full Privacy Policy.
Can others see my browsing history?
No. Your browsing history, starred items, and preferences are stored locally in your browser. We cannot see them, and they're never transmitted to our servers. Only you have access to this data on your device.
How do I delete my data?
Since data is stored locally:
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature
  • Select "All time" for the time range
  • Ensure "Cookies and site data" is checked
This will remove all Dirty4 data from your device.
How do I opt out of ads?
We use Google AdSense for advertising. To control ad personalization:
  • Visit Google Ads Settings
  • Turn off "Ad Personalization"
  • Use browser extensions like uBlock Origin (may affect site functionality)
Note: Ads help keep Dirty4 free for everyone!
