About Dirty4

Revolutionary Anime Art Gallery & Archival Platform

Our Mission

Dirty4 was created with a simple yet ambitious goal: to provide the best possible experience for browsing, discovering, and archiving anime and gaming artwork. We believe that digital art deserves a platform that combines beautiful design with powerful functionality, making art discovery both effortless and enjoyable.

In a world where digital art is often scattered across countless platforms with clunky interfaces and limited search capabilities, we saw an opportunity to do better. Dirty4 represents our commitment to creating a professional-grade art gallery that respects both the art and the artists who create it.

Our Story

2024 - The Beginning

Dirty4 started as a personal project born from frustration with existing art gallery platforms. We wanted something faster, more beautiful, and more user-friendly.

Early 2025 - Public Launch

After months of development and refinement, we launched Dirty4 to the public. The response from the community exceeded our expectations.

Mid 2025 - Major Updates

Implemented real API integrations with Safebooru and Danbooru, added mobile optimizations, and introduced advanced archival features.

Late 2025 - Community Growth

Launched our blog featuring digital art insights, tutorials, and artist spotlights. The Dirty4 community continues to grow daily.

Today - Continuous Innovation

We continue to improve Dirty4 based on user feedback, adding new features and optimizing performance. The journey has just begun.

Our Values

User Experience First

Every decision we make prioritizes the user experience. From our glassmorphism design to our lightning-fast search, we obsess over making Dirty4 a joy to use.

Respect for Artists

We deeply respect digital artists and their work. While we aggregate content from public sources, we always maintain proper attribution and encourage users to support the artists they love.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy matters. We collect minimal data, store preferences locally, and are transparent about our practices. Read our comprehensive Privacy Policy to learn more.

Technology & Innovation

Dirty4 is built using modern web technologies to ensure fast, reliable performance:

  • Progressive Web App (PWA): Works offline and can be installed on any device
  • Glassmorphism Design: Cutting-edge visual design that's both beautiful and functional
  • API Integrations: Real-time data from Danbooru, Safebooru, and other public galleries
  • Local-First Architecture: Your data stays on your device for privacy and performance
  • Responsive Design: Flawless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • Optimized Performance: Sub-100ms search times and lightning-fast page loads

Content & Sources

Dirty4 aggregates artwork from public art galleries and repositories, including:

  • Danbooru: One of the largest anime-style image boards with millions of tagged images
  • Safebooru: Family-friendly version of the popular imageboard
  • Public Galleries: Various other publicly accessible art repositories

All content displayed on Dirty4 is sourced from these public repositories. We do not host the images ourselves; we simply provide a better interface to browse and discover them. We respect copyright and intellectual property—if you're an artist and have concerns about your work, please contact us.
